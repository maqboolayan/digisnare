The purpose

Leads is an IT training centre hyper-focused on transforming students into ultra-competent engineers in the fields of IT, networking, and computer hardware.

Contrary to popular belief, the skills that you have in a discipline are far more valuable than a degree in that discipline. We founded Leads as a result of this epiphany.

Today, over a decade after its founding, Leads. remains a trailblazer in arming young minds with precise skills that make them prized engineers in the industry..

THE LEADS EXPERIENCES

Leads is an IT training centre hyper-focused on How can we provide maximum value to our students in the shortest time possible? At Leads, this is the one question that we constantly ask ourselves.

Technology is always evolving at a staggering pace. Our courses and our teaching methodologies must keep up with it if we are to bring out the all-star engineers in our students.

Hence, our courses are designed based on two fundamental rules. Firstly, they must provide a strong. foundation in the respective subjects. Secondly, they must equip the students with the necessary tools so that they can pursue new heights even after their placements.
